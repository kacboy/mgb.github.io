Wideshot

Magazine Design Concept

Wideshot is a magazine focusing on independent, hobbyist, and student filmmaking that I created as part of a senior publishing course at Simon Fraser University. Designed with a unique 2.39:1 CinemaScope-inspired size, the magazine prioritizes bold visuals, minimalistic typography, and layout choices that emphasize the imagery. Starting with a clear editorial mandate allowed for an unconventional magazine design with a polished visual identity.
