Alana

Connected Home Platform

Plasmatic Technologies' Alana Connected Home mobile app and dashboard system focusses on helping users manage their connected homes and gain insights from service providers. Building on initial mockups and plans created by the team, I refined and optimized the app's design to enhance usability and visual flexibility. Working closely with company leadership and developers, I helped implement a development workflow to ease the translation of my design mockups into fully functioning applications.
