EMUrgency

Android Game

EMUrgency is a space-themed Android game I developed using Processing 3â€™s Android mode. It's minimal touch controls and a zero-gravity mechanics were inspired by the endless runner genre. The game features procedural gameplay, a high-score system, and 8-bit style visuals created in Photoshop and through code-generated effects. Developing EMUrgency provided valuable experience in adapting games for mobile platforms, though future improvements like functional sound effects, music, and additional features were never implemented. It was available publicly on the Play Store for a time, but I didn't maintain the app to keep up with Google's developer policy updates.
